privacy policy.

Last updated: 23 July 2026

This policy explains what personal information Apex ("we", "us", "our") collects through this website, how it is used, and the rights you have over it. Apex is a web design studio based in North Down, Northern Ireland.

01.Information we collect

We only collect information you choose to give us. When you submit the contact form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Any details you include in your message

We do not run advertising trackers or sell data to third parties.

02.How we use it

Information submitted through the contact form is used solely to reply to your enquiry and, if we go on to work together, to deliver the project we agree on. We will not add you to a marketing list without your clear agreement.

03.Legal basis

Under UK GDPR, we process your information on the basis of legitimate interest — responding to an enquiry you have chosen to send us — and, where a project proceeds, on the basis of performance of a contract.

04.Who else is involved

This site uses a small number of third-party services to function:

  • Formspree — processes contact form submissions and forwards them to our inbox.
  • GitHub Pages — hosts this website.
  • Google Fonts — delivers the typefaces used on this site.

Each of these providers has its own privacy policy governing how it handles data.

05.How long we keep it

Enquiry emails are kept only as long as needed to deal with your enquiry and any resulting work, plus a reasonable period for our own records. You can ask us to delete them sooner at any time.

06.Cookies

This website does not set its own tracking or advertising cookies. Third-party services used on the site may set cookies of their own, governed by their respective policies.

07.Your rights

Under UK data protection law you have the right to request a copy of the personal information we hold about you, ask us to correct it, ask us to delete it, or object to how we use it. To exercise any of these rights, email apexdesignni@gmail.com.

If you are unhappy with how we have handled your information, you may also complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO), the UK's data protection regulator.

08.Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes take effect once published on this page.

09.Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to apexdesignni@gmail.com.
